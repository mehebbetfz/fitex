# Privacy Policy — FitEx

**Last updated: April 5, 2026**

FitEx ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the FitEx mobile application.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### Account Information
- **Name** — used to personalize your profile
- **Email address** — used for account creation, login, and verification codes

### Fitness & Health Data
- **Workout history** — exercises, sets, reps, weight, duration
- **Body measurements** — weight, height, BMI, and other body metrics you enter manually
- **Personal records** — your best results per exercise

### Usage Data
- **App interactions** — features used, screens visited (for improving app performance)

### Technical Data
- **Crash reports** — anonymous error logs to fix bugs

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- Create and manage your account
- Sync your fitness data across devices
- Display your progress, stats, and analytics
- Send email verification codes and security notifications
- Improve app performance and fix issues
- Provide customer support

We do **not** sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for advertising purposes.

---

## 3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on secure servers hosted by Hetzner (Germany, EU). We use industry-standard encryption (HTTPS/TLS) for all data transmission. Passwords are hashed using bcrypt and never stored in plain text.

---

## 4. Third-Party Services

FitEx uses the following third-party services:

| Service | Purpose |
|---------|---------|
| **Google Sign-In** | Optional authentication |
| **Apple Sign-In** | Optional authentication |
| **MongoDB Atlas** | Cloud database storage |
| **Expo / EAS** | App infrastructure and updates |
| **Apple App Store** | In-app purchases and subscriptions |

Each of these services has its own privacy policy. We recommend reviewing them separately.

---

## 5. In-App Purchases & Subscriptions

FitEx offers optional premium subscriptions. All payments are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not store or process your payment card information.

You can manage or cancel your subscription at any time through your Apple ID settings.

---

## 6. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active. You can request deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting us at the email below. Data will be deleted within 30 days of the request.

---

## 7. Children's Privacy

FitEx is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

---

## 8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

- **Access** your personal data
- **Correct** inaccurate data
- **Delete** your account and all associated data
- **Export** your fitness data

To exercise these rights, contact us at: **support@fitex.app**
